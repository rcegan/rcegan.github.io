title: “‘Attempted UAC Bypass via iscsicpl.exe’ Sentinel detection” date: 2022-07-20T00:00:00Z draft: false The UAC Bypass I was tipped off to the UAC bypass by this post from Will Dormann. I quickly found another tweet by @nas_bench with a Sigma rule to detect it. I jumped on Uncoder and generated the below query: DeviceImageLoadEvents | where (InitiatingProcessFolderPath =~ @"C:\Windows\SysWOW64\iscsicpl.exe" and not (FolderPath contains @"C:\Windows\" and FolderPath contains "iscsiexe.dll")) I’ve created a KQL file with entity mappings on my github.
